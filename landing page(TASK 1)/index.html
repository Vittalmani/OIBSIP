ABOUT US

Little Lemon is a cozy Italian restaurant that offers a variety of traditional Italian dishes with a modern twist. The restaurant is perfect for a romantic date night or a casual dinner with friends and family. The menu at Little Lemon features classic Italian dishes like pasta, pizza, and risotto, all made with fresh ingredients and prepared by skilled chefs. In addition to the savory options, the restaurant also offers a range of desserts like tiramisu and panna cotta that are sure to satisfy any sweet tooth. With its warm atmosphere and delicious food, Little Lemon is a must-visit for anyone looking to enjoy authentic Italian cuisine.
